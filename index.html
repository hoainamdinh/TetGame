📖 How to Play

🟢 Round 1: The Double Scramble

Unscramble letters to form Tet-related 2-word phrases. 10 points per correct answer.

🟡 Round 2: Tet Visual Riddles

2 hints per question. Correct after Hint 1 = 20 pts, after Hint 2 = 10 pts.

🟠 Round 3: The Secret Cipher

Decode numbers to letters (A=1, B=2…). 30 points per correct answer.

🔴 Round 4: The Lucky Envelope

Pick an envelope, answer correctly to win random hidden points (10-50). Option to Double your bet!
